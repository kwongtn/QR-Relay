Hi. Welcome to QR-Cottendance. Which end are you at?

Tutorial:

Assuming we have 2 parties, A and B. Where A is the giving party and B is the receiving party.
Giving party means that the party that scans or streams the QR code.
Receiving party means that the party that displays the QR code on another device.

Tutorial as follows:
  1. Person A goes to the giving end page to capture the QR code.
  2. On top of the page, if connection is established you will be given a sessionID. The sessionID is to be sent to the other party. Or you can key in your customID and give that instead.
  3. You can key in a custom key to send, or scan from webcam. Just keep the camera pointed at the code so that any updates will be instantly transmitted.
  4. Person B goes to the receiving end page to obtain the QR code.
  5. You then key in the sessionID to obtain the QR code.
